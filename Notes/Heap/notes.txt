For heaps you can get the left, right and parent of a node by doing the following:
left = 2i + 1
right = 2i + 2 
parent = [(i - 1) // 2]

heapq.nlargest O(nlogk)
heapq.nsmallest O(nlogk)
heapq.heapreplace | O(logk)
heapq.heappushpop
heapq.heappop + heapq.heappush | O(2logk)
heapq.merge

strategies:
1. Only keep heap size of k, for each new entrant not in k, skip or replace
2. Utilise max heap by inverting the values in your heap

notable algorithms:
1. Quickselect
2. Quicksort
