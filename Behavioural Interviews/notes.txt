Tell me about a time you had conflict?
- Answer needs to be high stakes:
    for example in an argument of space v tabs and selecting the right payment integration for the system
    the latter has better odds of being taken serious
-  The question is aimed at finding out if you avoid or embrace conflict
- Tell stories where you were right
- Express sympathy for the other persons POV
- Avoid villifying your opponent and making them look stupid 
- profer solution that offer long lasting solution to a recurring problem
- Tell the important part of the story
- bring along relevant data when standing your point

Prepare for follow ups 
- How did you measure success 
- What would you do different 
- Tell me about the challenges you faced while working on this project

Use the CARL method 
C - Context 
A - Action 
R - Results 
L - Learning

Time management 
Context [10%]
Actions [60%]
Result [20%]
Learning [10%]
